# Kairō — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: June 18, 2026_

Kairō is built privacy-first. **Your memories never leave your device.**

## The short version
- **No accounts. No servers. No tracking. No ads. No analytics.**
- Everything you capture — voice notes, text, transcripts, and the AI's answers —
  is created and stored **entirely on your iPhone**.
- Kairō makes **no network connections** to send your personal data anywhere.

## What Kairō stores, and where
All of the following is kept **locally on your device** and is never uploaded:
- Your check-ins (text and transcribed voice notes)
- The AI-generated answers, review cards, and weekly digests derived from them
- Usage state such as streaks and review schedules

Deleting the app removes all of this data from your device.

## Microphone & Speech Recognition
- The **microphone** is used only while you are recording a voice check-in.
- Audio is **transcribed on your device** using Apple's on-device Speech
  Recognition. Your recordings are **not** sent to Kairō or any third party.

## On-device AI
Kairō's answers, summaries, and recall prompts are generated by **Apple's
on-device Foundation Models** (and on-device embeddings) on supported devices.
Your memories are used **only on your device** to produce these results and are
never transmitted off the device for processing.

## Health-related information
Some notes you write may relate to your health (food, sleep, symptoms, etc.).
This information is treated like all other memories: **stored only on your
device, never collected or shared.**

## Data we collect about you
**None.** Kairō does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data.
(For Apple's App Privacy label: *Data Not Collected*.)

## Third parties
Kairō does not include third-party advertising, analytics, or tracking SDKs.

## Children
Kairō is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data
from anyone — because it does not collect data at all.

## Changes
If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new date.
