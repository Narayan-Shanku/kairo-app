Kairō

Your second memory — a voice-first, privacy-first personal AI for iPhone.

Speak or type a short daily check-in. Kairō remembers it, organizes it by life area, and lets you ask questions about your own life — with answers grounded in your own words, cited by date. Your memories stay on your iPhone.
