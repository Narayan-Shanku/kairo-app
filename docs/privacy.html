Kairō — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 18, 2026

Kairō is built privacy-first. Your memories never leave your device.

The short version

  • No accounts. No servers. No tracking. No ads. No analytics.
  • Everything you capture — voice notes, text, transcripts, and the AI's answers — is created and stored entirely on your iPhone.
  • Kairō makes no network connections to send your personal data anywhere.

What Kairō stores, and where

All of the following is kept locally on your device and is never uploaded:

  • Your check-ins (text and transcribed voice notes)
  • AI-generated answers, review cards, and weekly digests derived from them
  • Usage state such as streaks and review schedules

Deleting the app removes all of this data from your device.

Microphone & Speech Recognition

The microphone is used only while you record a voice check-in. Audio is transcribed on your device using Apple's on-device Speech Recognition and is not sent to Kairō or any third party.

On-device AI

Kairō's answers, summaries, and recall prompts are generated by Apple's on-device Foundation Models and on-device embeddings on supported devices. Your memories are used only on your device and are never transmitted off the device for processing.

Health-related information

Some notes may relate to your health (food, sleep, symptoms). This is treated like all other memories: stored only on your device, never collected or shared.

Data we collect about you

None. Kairō does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data. (Apple App Privacy label: Data Not Collected.)

Third parties

Kairō includes no third-party advertising, analytics, or tracking SDKs.

Children

Kairō is not directed at children and does not knowingly collect data from anyone — because it does not collect data at all.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new date.
