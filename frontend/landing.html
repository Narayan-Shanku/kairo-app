The problem

You already figured it out once. Then you forgot why it worked.

A health habit that finally worked. A study method that produced results. A debugging approach that fixed a 2 AM outage. Within days, the reasoning fades — even when the outcome is remembered. Context is lost, mistakes repeat, and hard-won knowledge evaporates.

Generic AI knows everything about the world and nothing about your life. Notes apps store but can't reason. Screen recorders capture noise, not signal. Kairō closes that gap.

How it works

Three layers. One natural habit.

01

Capture

02

Every entry is chunked, embedded, and auto-tagged by life domain — Health, Career, Learning, Projects, Fitness, Finance, Relationships.

03

Recall

Ask anything in plain language. A retrieval pipeline answers from your own words — grounded, cited by date, never hallucinated.
